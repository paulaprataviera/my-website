ABOUT

hello, world!

my name is paula prataviera and I was born in Brazil but I am currently living in Amsterdam.
this is my first web project, conceived while studying the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

moving to a new country was incredibly important for me to re-evaluate my personal and professional journey so far - it gave me the opportunity to build up the courage to work very hard and develop the technical skills I always wanted to, but kept postponing for no good reason.

it's been almost 10 years since I decided to join the advertising and communication industry, and I've been on the client-facing side of the table every single time, but now I find myself more and more excited to learn about front and back-end development as well.
